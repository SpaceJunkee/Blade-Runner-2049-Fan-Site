Joi - The Most Realistic Hologram Ever

27/03/2049

What makes Joi so real

Joi is a flawlessy perfect holographic companion that adapts to your needs and lifestyle. You can customize its hair, eye colour, gender, ethnicity, skin colour, body type and face type. It has a variety of vocal intonations and can adopt accents. It requires a projecting device such as a ceiling-mounted projector for a single room, or a portable Emanator device that allows Joi to travel with the user portably.

Joi's hardware is able to extensively sense the environment it is in and can translate that into data for the artificial intelligence to "experience" life along with the user.

Jois primary role is to communicate with other entities, human, replicant or otherwise, in a manner representing the user's mate or companion. She records data memories throughout her life time, allowing her personality to change and adapt over time.



Joi's program and memories reside on a main console, which for personal units was located in the home, as well as on portable Emanators or other linked devices. Joi hardware had the ability to utilize communications networks to contact other entities on its own initiative and could be used to monitor or track Joi users.

Joi is a must have for anyone looking for that special someone. Whether you need a best friend, partner, a maid or for someone to simply cheer you up in the morning, Joi is the perfect partner for you.
