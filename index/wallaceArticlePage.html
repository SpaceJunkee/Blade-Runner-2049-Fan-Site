Who is Niander Wallace

Niander Wallace is a scientist, a replicant manufacturer, a technologist and the founding CEO of the replicant manufacturing company, Wallace Corporation.

Wallace's first move onto the world stage occurred after the Blackout brought the world into a state of crisis, with stock markets crashing and food shortages prevalent. He pioneered advancements into genetically modified food, essentially bringing an end to the global food crisis, ending his reclusiveness, and allowing his company to expand on Earth and onto off-world Colonies.

Three years later, the bankrupt Tyrell Corporation was bought out by Wallace, indicating Wallace's desire to continue replicant development despite the prohibition of replicant technology after the Blackout.



Wallace began to modify genetic engineering to produce replicants that were not only superior in strength and agility compared to their human creators, but also loyal beyond question. These Nexus-9 replicants were made legal after Wallace proved the new models were 100% obidient.

Niander Wallace is an extremely sophisticated and perceptive individual, possessing an incredibly high intellect and a calm demeanor but despite all of his sophistication, Wallace is determined to accomplish his goals regardless of the cost.
